Edwin's Car Hobby Site

Home

Welcome to my personal hobby site. Here is where you'll find more information about one of my hobbies that I enjoy. I currently own a 2015 Subaru BRZ and a 1999 Honda Civic Hatchback. Below You will find pictures of both of them.

About me (who)

My name is Edwin Jimenez. I've always loved cars. As a kid I played with Hot Wheels, played the Need for Speed video games, and watched the Fast and Furious movies. I really started getting into cars when I got my license and was deciding on what car to buy. During my teenage years I was able to work and modify my car with my friends and being able to buy aftermarket parts. Now as a college student I don't have as much time to do these things but I still love learning new things and being able to connect with people that have the same interest.

I currently only own two cars. I am currently using my BRZ as a daily since there is work that needs to be done to my Civic. The plan is to eventually have one daily and one project. In the future I would like to buy another car, that being either a BMW 330CI or a Honda Del Sol.

Best times (when)

One of best moments I had with my friends working on my BRZ with my friends and installing headers onto the car. Trying to put thermal wrap around the headers, taking off the OEM headers, and running the car without to see how it would sound without headers for a minute were some of the highlights of that night. After installing the headers I was final able to run the car.

One of my favorite moments with my Civic was when my girlfriend and I gave it a deep clean. Being a 1999 model, it needed some TLC inside and out. Stripping out the interior and seeing the bare metal was a unique experience. Power washing the carpets and steam cleaning the plastics was satisfying. Seeing all the dirt and stains come out made the car feel brand new.

Where

Below are some tracks that are near the Charlotte area. The first one is the Charlotte Motor Speedway. At the Speedway they have the "U-Drive Experience" which allows you to bring your own car around the track for a couple of laps. The second track is Carolina Motor sports Park. There are lots of activities to do here and they also allow you to bring your own car onto the track.

How

As my hobby grew I was able to learn new things about the automotive world. Being able to work on my own car has been a learning experience. Most of my knowledge has been hands on and simple installations. Looking at youtube videos and forums has been helpful in fixing mistakes and issues that occur. Having friends around with formal experience has also helped me better understand more concepts than what I would learn by myself.

One of the biggest challenges that I have faced doing an engine swap on my Civic. My friend and I worked together to pull out an engine and installing a new one.

Why

There are many reasons why I got into this hobby. To start of with I love the joy of driving. Being able to shift through every gear and take corners makes it fun and exciting. You don't need a fast car to have fun. Another reason I enjoy this is because I am able to do work on my cars and have something to show for. Whether its maintaining the car or adding modifications, you can always see results.
